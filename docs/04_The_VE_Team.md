## 4. The VE Team

### Team Composition

- A minimum of three (3) SANDARC-accredited VEs must be present. If the number of available VEs drops below three at any point during a session, all exam activity must stop immediately until a third VE is present.
- VEs may not administer exams to relatives. The FCC prohibits testing of a VE's spouse, children, grandchildren, stepchildren, parents, grandparents, stepparents, brothers, sisters, stepbrothers, stepsisters, aunts, uncles, nieces, nephews, and in-laws. [§97.509(d)]
- SANDARC accreditation is required of every VE participating. Non-SANDARC-accredited VEs may not participate in SANDARC sessions under any circumstances. VE's not accreditated may observe sessions ONLY.

### Team Roles

| Role      | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
|-----------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Team Lead | The Team Lead is the VE designated to manage the session and serve as the primary point of contact with SANDARC. The Team Lead is responsible for session integrity, test material security, and submitting session results to vec@sandarc.org. In FCC rules, this role is called the Session Manager (§97.513). |
| VEs       | VEs who proctor and observe applicants during the exam. Must hold the appropriate license class for the elements being administered.                                                                                                                                                                             |

>  **SANDARC POLICY:**

> - Only Team Owners and Co-Owners in ExamTools receive the Team Lead permission. Team Owners may add Co-Owners to their team at their discretion but must notify the VEC of additional Co-Owners so the VEC database can be updated.
> - For teams that will have multiple Team Leads for submitting sessions those members will also receive the Team Lead permission in Exam Tools but may not create new teams unless prior authorization is granted from SANDARC.
> - For online/remote sessions, the current SANDARC President and VEC Manager must be added to the ExamTools team as Co-Owners.
> - Only VEs who were physically or virtually present at the session may be listed on the session documents.
> - VEC Archive file from exam tools must not have its files tampered with in any way. Session integrity is one of highest priorities with SANDARC and ALL session submissions will be vetted for accuracy!

### SANDARC Branding & Other VEC Rules

>  **SANDARC POLICY:**

> - No branding of any other VEC organization is permitted at SANDARC exam sessions. Team Branding and Club Branding is always authorized!
> - Running concurrent sessions with another VEC at the same time and place is prohibited, except in cases of minor/youth reimbursement programs with other VECs.
> - When sessions with a different VEC are conducted at the same location on the same day, leave at least 1 hour between sessions.
